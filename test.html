Smart Campus
Lost & Found System

A centralized digital platform for students and staff to report, search, and claim lost items efficiently across campus.

Report Lost Items

Quickly report lost items with details, location, and description.

Submit Found Items

Found something? Log it and help reunite items with their owners.

Submit Claims

Identify your belongings and formally claim them through the system.

Admin Oversight

Full verification, audit logs, and claim management by administrators.

Upload Item Images

Users can upload items images for proper identification and verification.

Search Suggestions

Provide intelligent keyword-based search suggestions to help users to quickly locate lost and found belongings using various categories.

Every report is stored in MySQL, making item records, claim history, and admin decisions easy to track for an RDBMS project.

The system connects students, finders, and administrators through one workflow: report, browse, claim, verify, and resolve.
